Homestyle Potato Chips

Prep

Preperation Time: 40 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total: 60 minutes

Servings: approximately 7-8

Ingredients

  • 4 medieum potatoes, peeled and sliced paper thin
  • 3 tablespoons salt, add more to taste
  • 1 quart oil for deep frying

Directions

  1. Transfer potato slices into a large bowl of cold water as you slice them.
  2. Drain slices and rinse under cold water. Refill the bowl with water, add 3 tablespoons salt, and put slices back into the bowl. Let potatoes soak in the salty water for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Drain and rinse slices again. Pat dry.
  4. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 365 degrees F.(185 degrees C)
  5. Working in small batches, fry potato slices until golden. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Continue until all slices are fried.
  6. Season potato chips with additional salt or seasoning if desired.
