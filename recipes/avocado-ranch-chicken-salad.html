Avocado Ranch Chicken Salad

Prep

Preperation Time: 10 minutes

Wait Time: 30 minutes

Total: 40 minutes

Servings: approximately 4-5

Ingredients

  • 1 pound diced cooked chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup chopped cashews
  • 1/2 medieum avocado, diced
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons light ranch dressing
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons minced fresh dill
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Mix chicken, cashews, avocado, ranch dressing, dill, salt and pepper together in a bowl.
  2. Chill for at least 30 minutes prior to serving.
